Yunlong Mao

I am a research member of COSEC, led by Prof. Sheng Zhong. Before that, I was a visiting scholar at the College of William & Mary, working with Prof. Qun Li. I got my Ph.D. and B.S. degrees in Computer Science from Nanjing University in 2018 and 2013, respectively. My research interests include but are not limited to security, privacy, networks, machine learning, and blockchain.
